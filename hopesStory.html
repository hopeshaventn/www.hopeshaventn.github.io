Hope's Story

Hope McCoy

Hope was a joy-filled child. She loved and was loved by anyone who was lucky enough to know her. She loved spending time with her family and friends, playing outside, swimming, cheering on her older siblings, and being the “team hugger.” Hope loved music and dancing, sneaking marshmallows and chocolate kisses, and was known for her infectious giggle. Hope passed away in August 2019 at the age of 6. She was diagnosed with Xia-Gibbs Syndrome, a rare disorder affecting her speech and motor skills. The greatest lesson we learned from Hope: be with the people you love- in joy, in life, in silence, with love and acceptance. Just be with them, love them and let them know.
