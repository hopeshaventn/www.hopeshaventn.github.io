Frequently Asked Questions


Who can come?

Anyone and everyone! We welcome children ages 6 to 17 of any ability. Our wonderful team of nurses and volunteers will be trained to handle any needs on a case by case basis. If your child is outside of our recommended age range but is interested in attending, contact us at info@hopeshaventn.com

Where are you located?

We currently have land on Tracy Mountain (near Foster Falls.) However, we will be renting a campground in Chattanooga until we are able to raise the funds to build our permanent home.

What will my child be doing?

We offer a wide range of activities like arts and crafts, basketball, archery, climbing, swimming, nightly parties, and more! Each camper can participate in as much or as little as they choose. At Hope's Haven, you are not defined by your disability. All activities will be adapted so that campers of every ability can participate.
