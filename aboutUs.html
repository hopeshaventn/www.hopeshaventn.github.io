About Us

WELCOME TO HOPE'S HAVEN

Hope's Haven is a Christ-centered summer camp for children with special needs and disabilities. We provide an environment for campers to grow in Christ, in relationships, and in confidence.

MEET THE TEAM


Abbey Sutherland


Abbey Sutherland is our founder and executive director. She is an accounting and finance major at UTC, but has a passion for working with children with special needs. Abbey has been serving this community since she was 16 years old. She is looking forward to meeting all of our volunteers and campers and cannot wait to see how God uses Hope’s Haven for His glory.

Theresa Nix


Theresa is the mother of a son with Down Syndrome, Everett. She has years of teaching experience and currently runs Downside Up- a parent-directed non-profit providing support, education, and encouragement for parents, families and others who love people with special needs. We are so glad to have Theresa serving on our board to share her wisdom as a mother and as a CEO!

Nicolle McCoy


Nicolle is the mother of Ava, Matthew, Connor, and Hope. She and her husband, Scott, run a non-profit dedicated to serving individuals with special needs, Hope Included. Nicolle also works as a controller at New Blue Construction. We are so glad that Nicolle is utilizing her business knowledge and passion for advocacy by serving on our board!

Susan Sutherland


Susan has been in education for over 20 years. She is a teacher at Chattanooga Christian School and currently has Theresa's son, Everett, in her second grade class. Susan will be acting as our "camp mom" and cannot wait to meet all of her future campers!

Amy Gouger


Amy is a partner at Henderson + Gouger, CPA. Hope's Haven is thankful to call Amy our treasurer. She brings over 20 years of public accounting experience to our board. Service is a large part of Amy's life, as she is also involved with the Children's Advocacy Center.

Emma Blackburn


Emma is excited to serve as one of our camp nurses! She graduated as an RN in 2020 from Chattanooga State. Emma currently works at Erlanger Hospital in the NICU. She has a love for children and service. We are so happy that she is choosing to use her gifts with us at Hope's Haven!
